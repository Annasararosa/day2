Kerala, a state in southwestern India, is known for its picturesque landscapes, backwaters, and diverse culture. With Thiruvananthapuram as its capital, the state boasts a high literacy rate and is a popular tourist destination. Kerala's economy is driven by agriculture, manufacturing, and services, with a significant contribution from remittances. The region has a rich cultural heritage, celebrated through traditional arts, festivals, and Ayurveda. Renowned for its social indicators, Kerala emphasizes education, healthcare, and sustainable development.
